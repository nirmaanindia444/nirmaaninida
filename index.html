About Us...

Since inception in year 2012, from the city of Indore, Madhya Pradesh, Nirmaan India has charted its own growth story, based on the strong belief of unfailing customer orientation. Today, the organization led by Nikhil Garg, 32 stands for its commitment and agility in the construction equipment machinery market of Madhya Pradesh & having good Experience of sell the construction equipment like Excavator, Loader, Grader, Portable Compressors, Skid Steer Loaders, Batching Plant, Pumps Etc.

With a diversified expertise of more than 8 years in the field of building materials, construction equipments & concrete machineries spares, Nirmaan India is well on its way to achieve its vision of being the organization known for its customer centricity. Backed by a progressive track record in the expansive and competitive markets like Madhya Pradesh, Nirmaan India is prepared to emerge as a dependable partner.

OUR SERVICES

High-Rise Construction & Lifting Solutions

We specialize in complete high-rise building solutions, offering advanced equipment for safe, fast, and efficient vertical construction. From material lifting to custom hoist systems, our technology ensures smooth movement of heavy loads across floors—making even the tallest projects manageable.

Concrete Pumping & RMC Machinery

Our concrete pumping systems and RMC machines are built for reliability, precision, and performance. Whether it's high-rise pours or large-scale mixing, we deliver consistent quality, optimal flow, and minimal downtime—keeping your project on track from the first pour to the last.

Service, Support & Maintenance

Beyond machinery, we provide expert maintenance, on-site support, and end-to-end service to ensure your equipment performs at its best—always. From preventive care to emergency repairs, our team keeps your construction operations running safely and smoothly.
